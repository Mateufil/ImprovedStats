# <cj>Improved Stats</c>
This mod improves the Stats Menu to make it look better and include new stats!

It adds new stats such as collected icons, completed weekly demons, total completed quests, achievements and more!

<cc>More stats may be added with updates!</c>

## <cy>Stats</c>
List of stats, new stats are marked with <cj>blue</c>.

To access some extra stats you need to press the stat with a <cg>+ symbol</c> next to them!

### Basic
- Jumps
- Attempts
- Official Levels
	- <cj>Main, Tower, Meltdown, World, SubZero, Other</c>
- User Levels
- Insane levels
- Demon Levels

### Collectibles
- Stars
- <cj>Moons</c>
- Secret Coins
- User Coins
	- <cj>Unverified Coins</c>
- Collected Diamonds
	- <cj>Diamond Shards</c>
- Collected Orbs
	- <cj>Current Orbs</c>

### Completions
- Daily Levels
- <cj>Weekly Demons</c>
- <cj>Event Levels</c>
	- <cj>Auto (1*), Easy (2*), Normal (3*), Hard (4*), Hard (5*)</c>
	- <cj>Harder (6*), Harder (7*), Insane (8*), Insane (9*), Demon (10*)</c>
- Map Packs
- Gauntlets
	- <cj>Completed Levels, Completed Demons</c>
- List Rewards

### Icons
- <cj>Icons</c>
	- <cj>Cubes, Ships, Balls, UFOs, Waves</c>
	- <cj>Robots, Spiders, Swings, Jetpacks</c>
- <cj>Colors</c>
	- <cj>Main Colors, Secondary Colors</c>
- <cj>Special Items</c>
	- <cj>Trails, Ship Fires, Animations, Death Effects</c>
- <cj>Bought Items</c>
	- <cj>Normal, Secret, Community, Mechanic, Diamond, Paths</c>

### Other
- Liked/Disliked Levels
- Rated Levels
- Destroyed Players
- <cj>Achievements</c>
	- <cj>Main, Meltdown, World, SubZero</c>
- <cj>Quests</c>
	- <cj>10 Diamonds, 15 Diamonds, 20 Diamonds</c>

## <co>Pinning Stats</c>
If you want to keep track of some stats you are interested in, you can <cg>pin them!</c>

To pin a stat you need to go into the info menu by pressing a stat, and then press the <cg>Pin</c> button.

Pinned stats appear on the <cj>first page</c> of the <cl>Stats Menu</c>.
