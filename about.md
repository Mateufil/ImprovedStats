# <cj>Improved Stats</c>
This mod improves the Stats Menu to make it look better and include new stats!

<cc>More stats may be added with updates!</c>

## <cy>Stats</c>
List of stats, new stats are marked with <cl>blue</c>.
- Jumps
- Attempts
- Official Levels
- User Levels
- Insane levels
- Demon Levels
- Stars
- <cl>Moons</c>
- Secret Coins
- User Coins
- Collected Diamonds
- Collected Orbs
- Daily Levels
- <cl>Weekly Demons</c>
- <cl>Event Levels</c>
- Map Packs
- Gauntlets
	- <cl>Completed Levels</c>
	- <cl>Completed Demons</c>
- List Rewards
- Liked/Disliked Levels
- Rated Levels
- Destroyed Players
- <cl>Achievements</c>
	- <cl>Main</c>
	- <cl>Meltdown</c>
	- <cl>World</c>
	- <cl>SubZero</c>
- <cl>Quests</c>
	- <cl>10 Diamonds</c>
	- <cl>15 Diamonds</c>
	- <cl>20 Diamonds</c>
